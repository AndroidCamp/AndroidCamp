Android Camp

Tue July 12, 2016

@ United Nations

An community-run and not-for-profit conference focused on the open source Android platform.

Android Camp has two sessions - a small daytime TRAINING session and large cross-community evening TECH TALKS session.

The training session targets absolute beginners. You will learn to work with Android Studio to build your first Android app.
All attendees are required to bring laptops (and chargers).

Both sessions will be hosted at the United Nations HQ in NYC
